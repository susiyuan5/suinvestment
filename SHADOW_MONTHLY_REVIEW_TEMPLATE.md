# Shadow Monthly Review Template

This template is for human review of research-only Shadow Observation outputs. It is not trading advice and does not activate any symbol.

## Purpose

Review whether the 12 monitored symbols continue to show research value across multiple monthly/manual observation runs.

## Evidence Required

- At least 8 unique observation runs.
- At least 8 calendar weeks of observation history.
- Repeated same-day observations do not count as monthly evidence.
- No risk warnings.
- No missing data warnings.
- No candidate degradation.
- Valid archive integrity report.
- Human review before any deeper sandbox-only step.
